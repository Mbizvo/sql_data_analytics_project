# Analysis Summary

This project provides a complete SQL-based analytics framework for exploring, measuring, and understanding business performance.

---

## Key Analytical Areas

### 1. Data Exploration
Initial analysis identifies:
- Data distributions
- Date ranges
- Table sizes and relationships

This ensures data quality before deeper analysis.

---

### 2. Measures & Metrics
Core business KPIs are calculated including:
- Revenue
- Orders
- Customers
- Product performance
- Growth rates

These metrics form the foundation for management reporting.

---

### 3. Time-Series Analysis
The project analyzes:
- Monthly and daily sales trends
- Growth and decline patterns
- Seasonality
- Cumulative performance

This helps understand how the business evolves over time.

---

### 4. Ranking & Performance
Products and customers are ranked by:
- Revenue
- Volume
- Contribution

This highlights top performers and underperformers.

---

### 5. Segmentation
Customers and products are grouped into segments based on:
- Sales value
- Purchase behavior
- Contribution to revenue

Segmentation enables targeted business strategies.

---

### 6. Reporting
Final SQL scripts produce ready-to-use reports:
- Customer performance report
- Product performance report

These outputs are suitable for dashboards, Excel, or BI tools.

---

## Business Value

This analytics framework allows decision-makers to:
- Track financial performance
- Identify growth opportunities
- Understand customer behavior
- Optimize product strategy
- Support data-driven decisions
